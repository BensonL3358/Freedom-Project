Paul Allen is an American businessman and he is most known for being the cofounder of Microsoft Corporation.

Paul Allen was a main contributor to the world of technology because he cofounded the Microsoft Corporation. The Microsoft Corporation is an American technology company that produces computers and personal items for computers. Microsoft has provided many people across the world to access to technology and allowing them to learn from the Internet.
